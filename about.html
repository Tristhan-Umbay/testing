About Nabunturan National Comprehensive High School

Nabunturan National Comprehensive High School (NNCHS) is one of the leading secondary schools in Davao de Oro, dedicated to providing quality education and holistic development to students. The school promotes academic excellence, creativity, and innovation through various programs, including Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM), as well as Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS).

NNCHS is strategically located along the National Highway in Nabunturan, the capital municipality of Davao de Oro. The school is easily accessible via public transportation, making it convenient for students from surrounding barangays. The campus features modern classrooms, science laboratories, a well-stocked library, a computer lab, and extensive sports facilities including a covered court and a football field. The school environment promotes both academic learning and extracurricular activities, encouraging students to develop leadership, teamwork, and social responsibility.

The surrounding area of NNCHS is known for its vibrant community, local businesses, and scenic landscapes. Nabunturan itself is a hub for cultural and economic activities in Davao de Oro, providing students opportunities to engage in community outreach programs and experiential learning outside the classroom.

School Location

Nabunturan National Comprehensive High School (NNCHS) is located along the National Highway in the heart of Nabunturan, Davao de Oro. This prime location provides easy access for students coming from nearby barangays and makes the school well-connected to the town’s commercial areas, community centers, and public transportation routes.

The school is surrounded by scenic landscapes and a vibrant community, making it an ideal environment for learning and growth. Visitors can easily explore the campus and its facilities, while students benefit from nearby amenities, including libraries, sports complexes, and cultural hubs.

A NNCHS Research Hub

The NNCHS Research Hub is a digital platform designed to help students explore, create, and share their research projects. It serves as a collaborative space for learning, guidance, and inspiration. With resources, examples, and AI-powered support, the Research Hub encourages students to enhance their analytical and critical thinking skills while promoting a culture of inquiry and discovery.
