About Nabunturan National Comprehensive High School

Nabunturan National Comprehensive High School (NNCHS) is a leading secondary school in Davao de Oro, offering quality education and holistic development. The school fosters academic excellence, creativity, and innovation through programs like STEM, HUMSS, and more. Facilities include science labs, a library, sports arenas, and digital classrooms.

Founded in the 1990s, NNCHS has consistently ranked among top schools in the region, earning awards for academic performance, research competitions, and community engagement.

School Location

Nabunturan National Comprehensive High School

Located in Nabunturan, Davao de Oro, the school is easily accessible via the National Highway and serves students from surrounding municipalities.

A NNCHS Research Hub

The NNCHS Research Hub is a digital platform to help students explore, create, and share research projects. It provides guidance, AI-powered support, and collaborative spaces to enhance critical thinking and innovation.

Key Features:

  • Interactive project submission with step-by-step guidance
  • Access to tutorials, examples, and research templates
  • AI-powered assistance for methodology and analysis
  • Progress tracking, quizzes, and real-time feedback
  • Collaborative spaces for student brainstorming
  • Showcase of exemplary student projects and competitions

Purpose:

To cultivate research, critical thinking, creativity, and innovation among students, preparing them for higher education and professional growth.

Published:

Launched in 2025, the hub provides interactive tools to empower NNCHS students in achieving academic excellence.
