Senior High School Strands

Explore the academic and technical-vocational strands offered at NNCHS. Each strand helps students build essential skills and prepare for their future careers.

STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics)

Focuses on scientific and mathematical concepts, preparing students for courses like engineering, medicine, and computer science.

HUMSS (Humanities and Social Sciences)

Ideal for students who enjoy writing, public speaking, and understanding society. Prepares them for careers in education, law, or communication.

ABM (Accountancy, Business, and Management)

Develops leadership, financial literacy, and business management skills for students aspiring to be entrepreneurs or business professionals.

GAS (General Academic Strand)

Provides a balanced curriculum for students who are still exploring their future paths, offering subjects across all disciplines.

TVL (Technical-Vocational-Livelihood)

Offers hands-on technical training for students aiming to work immediately after senior high school in various industries.

ICT (Information and Communications Technology)

Builds computer, programming, and networking skills for those interested in technology, digital systems, and software development.

AFA (Agri-Fishery Arts)

Focuses on agriculture and aquaculture, equipping students with practical knowledge in sustainable farming and fishery practices.
